ğŸ”„ How to Use

  • Add 1-2 tablespoons of grains to 2-4 cups of milk
  • Cover with breathable cloth and ferment for 24-48 hours
  • Strain the kefir grains from the fermented milk
  • Reuse grains for next batch immediately
  • Store extra grains in milk in refrigerator
  • Grains will multiply with regular use
