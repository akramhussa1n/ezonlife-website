🔄 How to Use

  • Add 1-2 tablespoons of grains to 2-4 cups of milk
  • Cover with breathable cloth and ferment for 24-48 hours
  • Strain the kefir grains from the fermented milk
  • Reuse grains for next batch immediately
  • Store extra grains in milk in refrigerator
  • Grains will multiply with regular use

💡 Care Instructions

  • Use glass or plastic containers (no metal)
  • Keep at room temperature during fermentation
  • Use fresh milk for best results
  • Strain gently to avoid damaging grains
  • Rinse occasionally with filtered water
  • Share extra grains with friends and family

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Grains are living organisms - handle with care
  • May take 2-3 batches to reach optimal activity
  • Temperature affects fermentation time
  • Grains may change appearance over time
  • Can be frozen for long-term storage
  • Contact us for any issues or questions
